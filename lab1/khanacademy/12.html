Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Bacon (or pancetta) 200g
Eggs 4 (large)
Parmesan cheese 100g (grated)
Black pepper To taste
Salt To taste

Step 1: Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Reserve some pasta water before draining.

Step 2: In a pan, cook the bacon or pancetta until crispy. Remove from heat and set aside.

Step 3: In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated parmesan cheese, and black pepper.

Step 4: Toss the drained spaghetti in the bacon pan and add the egg mixture, stirring quickly to create a creamy sauce. Add pasta water if needed to adjust consistency.

Step 5: Serve immediately, topped with extra parmesan cheese and black pepper.

Chocolate Cake

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Cocoa powder 3/4 cup
Sugar 1 1/2 cups
Baking powder 1 1/2 teaspoons
Eggs 2
Butter 1/2 cup
Milk 1 cup

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch cake pans.

Step 2: In a large bowl, mix together flour, cocoa powder, sugar, and baking powder.

Step 3: Add eggs, butter, and milk. Mix until smooth.

Step 4: Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pans and bake for 30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.

Step 5: Let the cakes cool before frosting and serving.
